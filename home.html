Why Uncommon?
UNCOMMON App is a New York-based boutique educational consulting service. Founded by Dr. Elena Polenova in 2017, UNCOMMON App has helped more than 150 students earn acceptances to Ivy League schools, competitive private universities, small liberal arts colleges, and outstanding public institutions as well as graduate and medical school admissions.

Finding the right match. Uncommon Appoperates behind a crucial principle: it is far more important to find the right match than to simply win a slot at any one institution. The volatile landscape of college admissions is intimidating, and at times unpredictable and incomprehensible. In moving away from the clichés of “target” institutions and “safety” schools, Elena helps reorient students with a very simple question: where would you be happy to attend? By keeping a collective focus on finding a sense of belonging, Elena sets herself apart as a true ally to her students (and, often, an unofficial therapist to their parents).

Customizing our approach. The success of UNCOMMON App lay in its name. Elena rejects a uniform methodology and adopts a highly customized approach to each client based on their personality, aspirations, and family background. The heart of her work is developing close, trusting relationships with students from early in their high school careers, and mapping intensely individualized programs catered exclusively to student success. Elena’s background in psychological research and career counseling has equipped her to help students better identify, understand, and articulate their own strengths. This confidence-building is key.

Earning confidence. Honesty and open communication are just as essential to UNCOMMON App’s success. Elena is direct and realistic, and prides herself on understanding her clients’ goals and fears equally. Her ability to strike a balance between pushing hard and encouraging introspection shows students how to stay confident and excited about what matters most to them.

Achieving results. Elena’s priority is guiding her students to their own paths. Her most impressive strength is in how close she gets to her clients. By placing as much emphasis on personality and happiness as she does on talent and ability, Elena always puts students first. In employing insight and empathy to achieve results, UNCOMMON App always delivers.

Our approach
Uncommon App’s signature comprehensive plan centers on building a long-term relationship with your student. This program is most effective when activated during the student’s sophomore year of high school, in preparation for choosing consequential junior year courses. In getting to know your student as early as possible, we will also get on track for competitive summer programs relevant to their college aspirations.
