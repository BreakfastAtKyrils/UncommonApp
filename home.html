Our process
Uncommon App’s signature comprehensive plan centers on building a long-term relationship with your student. This program is most effective when activated during the student’s sophomore year of high school, in preparation for choosing consequential junior year courses. In getting to know your student as early as possible, we will also get on track for competitive summer programs relevant to their college aspirations.
