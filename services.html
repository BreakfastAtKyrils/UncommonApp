We firmly believe that each student possesses a unique combination of personality traits, interests, and talents. However, from our experience and from psychological research we understand that young people often have problems recognizing and articulating their strengths in essays and interviews. This can hurt their applications. We help young people to identify their talents and teach them how to successfully describe them, effectively building their confidence well beyond successful college admission.

Whether you’re interested in our signature, long-term comprehensive plan or a more brief service like our Panic Button or Essay Rescue programs, we will get to know your student in the same way through a complimentary introduction meeting and an elaborate, research and analysis-based consultation:

Introduction
A complimentary 20-30 minute meeting to answer questions. For undergraduate students, this meeting will be conducted with parents only.
Consultation: Assessment and Recommendations
We'll start with learning about your student’s academic history and objectives and discussing current admissions landscapes. Our thorough evaluative consultation as a platform for the comprehensive plan. This assessment involves 2-5 hours of extensive research and test analysis. By admissions expert Elena. In familiarizing herself with your student, their talents and abilities, and their ambitions, Elena will be able to offer educated opinions and invaluable recommendations as they begin their journey into finding the right school, together.

COMPREHENSIVE PLAN

UNCOMMON App’s signature comprehensive plan is centered by building a long-term relationship with your student. This program is most effective when activated during the student’s sophomore year of high school, in preparation for choosing consequential junior year courses. In getting to know your student as early as possible, we will also get on track for competitive summer programs relevant to their college aspirations.

The comprehensive plan entails an extensive commitment and promises the best results. UNCOMMON App welcomes a select amount of new students into this exclusive program annually.


Learn more about our comprehensive plan

PANIC BUTTON

Some rising high school seniors find themselves overwhelmed and lost ahead of application season. To steer your student in the right direction, this short program will involve the main aspects of the evaluative consultation package, as well as a writing-intensive focus as we work together on developing and finishing application essays.

Please be advised that we take these students on a case-by-case basis.

ESSAY RESCUE

Admissions essays are often the most intimidating application element for students who struggle to put into words what is most important to them. UNCOMMON App’s team of essay editors is here to help your student connect their defining experiences with greater life themes by developing even the roughest ideas into a clean, compelling essay.
