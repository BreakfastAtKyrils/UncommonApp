Understanding our comprehensive plan

UNCOMMON App’s signature comprehensive plan is centered by building a long-term relationship with your student. This program is most effective when activated during the student’s sophomore year of high school, in preparation for choosing consequential junior year courses. In getting to know your student as early as possible, we will also get on track for competitive summer programs relevant to their college aspirations.

The comprehensive plan entails an extensive commitment and promises the best results. UNCOMMON App welcomes a select amount of new students into this exclusive program annually.
